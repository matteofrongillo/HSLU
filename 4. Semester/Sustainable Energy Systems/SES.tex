\part{Introduction to SES}
\section{Sustainability}
\subsection{Sustainable energy systems}
The energy system comprises all energy extraction, conversion, storage,
transmission, and distribution processes that deliver \textbf{final energy} to
the end-user.

\subsubsection{Environmental sustainability}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Addresses ecological issues such as reducing GHG emissions
  \item Objective: protect the planet and preserve natural resources
  \item Focuses on environmental quality and resource conservation
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Economic sustainability}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Concerns long-term economic performance
  \item Objective: ensure financial viability without exhausting resources
  \item Focuses on economic stability and business continuity
  \item Recent debates also consider degrowth and zero growth
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Social sustainability}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Promotes a stable, fair, and inclusive society
  \item Objective: safeguard human dignity and human rights across generations
  \item Focuses on poverty, work, and the fair distribution of social burdens
\end{itemize}
