\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Title}: Cold-water circulation with a heat pump
    \item \textbf{Supervisor}: Ludger Fischer
    \item \textbf{Industrial partner / CC}: R. Nussbaum AG / Patrik Zeiter
    \item \textbf{Description}:\\
    The project consists of a domestic water system with
    separate domestic cold water (DCW) and domestic hot water (DHW)
    distribution. The DHW side includes a circulation loop to
    maintain hygiene and allow disinfection against Legionella.
    The key idea is to create a closed DCW loop before the
    domestic distribution and use it as the circuit for a heat pump
    for heat extraction and DHW preparation.

    This configuration keeps the domestic distribution
    hygienically safe and assesses whether the cold-water loop
    can reliably support the heat pump for the production of
    domestic hot water. 
    
    The scope is to size the main hydraulic and thermal components
    and test the technical feasibility of the heat pump,
    including the evaporator, condenser, compressor, expansion
    valve, circulation pumps, and a possible storage connection.
\end{itemize}
